Temp Dev. Sites are used for faculty who want access to a site for an upcoming section prior to the three week live–site access date.

Content is based on a previous semesters site.

Students do not have access to Temp-Dev sites.

These sites EXPIRE three weeks after the official course start date and are removed from the Blackboard server.

TEMPORARY DEV. SITE REQUEST FORM
First Name: Enter your first name in this field.
Last Name: Enter your last name in this field.
HCNet ID: Enter your HCNet ID. Generally, it's your last name. It's the same ID that you use to log into BlackBoard.
Email: Enter your preferred email address.
For example, myemail@humber.ca
Course Number: Enter the course number without a space.
For example, ACCT108.
Content Base: Enter the BlackBoard Course Number you would like the temporary dev site's content to be based on. For example:

ACCT 108 - 33 (Jan 16 2009 - Apr 24 2009)
Semester: Please indicate the semester when your live site will begin. For example, Fall 2009, Winter 2009, or Summer 2009.
   
NOTE: These sites EXPIRE three weeks after the official course start date and are removed from the Blackboard server.
